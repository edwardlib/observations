Percentage of Shrimp in Shrimp Cocktail
---------------------------------------

Description
~~~~~~~~~~~

A numeric vector with 18 determinations by different laboratories of the
amount (percentage of the declared total weight) of shrimp in shrimp
cocktail.

Usage
~~~~~

::

    shrimp

~~~~~~
